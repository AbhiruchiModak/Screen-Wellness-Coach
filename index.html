Welcome to Screen Wellness Coach

This app helps you build healthy screen habits with reminders for blinking, posture, stretching, and screen breaks.

This short guide takes about 60 seconds and shows you exactly how everything works.

Skip guide

Your four reminder timers

Each card counts down to the next reminder. Use the - and + buttons to shorten or lengthen the interval. Flip the toggle to activate a reminder.

  • Blink — reminds you to blink every 20 seconds by default
  • Posture — nudges you to sit up every 5 minutes
  • Stretch — prompts a standing stretch every 20 minutes
  • Screen — tells you to look away every 20 minutes

Notification frequency

Each timer has a Frequency mode you can expand. Set how often (every N seconds) a reminder fires, and for how long (M seconds total window). The reminder repeats inside that window, then stops until the next cycle.

Example: remind every 30 s for the next 2 minutes — perfect for a persistent nudge without being overwhelming forever.

Notification actions

Every reminder notification carries two action buttons:

  • I understood — marks this reminder as done and turns off that timer until you re-enable it
  • Snooze 10 min — pauses that reminder for 10 minutes, then it resumes automatically

If you ignore a notification, reminders continue on schedule — you stay in control.

Camera posture monitor

Click Enable Camera to activate live monitoring. The app uses your webcam to check:

  • Screen distance — are you too close or too far from your monitor?
  • Eye level — is your screen at a healthy height for your neck?

A push notification fires if a problem persists. Your video never leaves your device.

Accessibility controls

The toolbar at the top of the page has two accessibility buttons available at all times:

  • High Contrast — switches to a high-contrast dark theme for improved readability
  • Larger Text — cycles through three font sizes (normal, large, extra large)
